About Us

Luke’s New York Bagels is a recent addition to the Gainesville community. Luke’s recently opened our first brick and mortar location. In the beginning, Luke Vescovi saw a need. He along with his parents are New Jersey transplants, having been brought here by the University of Florida. Luke’s mother also happens to have worked at a popular New Jersey bagel spot and developed a recipe 30 years in the making. A perfect storm was brewing and they were soon selling small batches of bagels out of the Halo Potato Donuts storefront on South Main Street. After some trial and error, but the small batches became bigger and bigger, and soon Luke’s was selling out of bagels within a couple hours. After a great deal of support from the community, Luke’s New York Bagels was able to open its own brick and mortar business in the former LEJ Pretzel location at 620 South Main Street.

Regardless of COVID19, the location does not offer indoor seating, and has limited outdoor tables. Luke’s New York Bagels serves out of a togo counter with a few onsite outdoor tables that are first come, first serve.

Luke’s New York Bagels offers authentic New York style bagels and different flavors of homemade creamcheese. These can be toasted to perfection onsite and served with creamcheese. The bagels are also sold untoasted by the dozen and half dozen to enjoy at home or to bring to an event. Eggs and cheese will be appearing on the menu in the near future.
